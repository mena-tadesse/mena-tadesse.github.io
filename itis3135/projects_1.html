Project Overview

The website serves as an informative hub for an auto shop. It outlines the organization's backstory and values, emphasizing its commitment to quality service. Visitors can easily navigate through our range of services and transparent pricing. I want to make it a user-friendly online appointment system, allowing effortless scheduling while specifying individual automotive requirements. The goal is to provide a seamless and engaging experience for all users. Intended users of the website.

Inteded Users: People that own vehicles.
