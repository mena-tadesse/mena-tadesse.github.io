About Me

Ever since I was young, I took interest in art. I would enjoy drawing pricesses on paper and hanging them up in my bedroom, creating funky pink bracelets with my sister, and finger painting clay mugs and bowls. I have so many fond memories with this activity, and will continue to make some more.

Lately, I've been trying to paint in watercolor and am looking into learning how to knit. I mean, wouldn't it be so cool to make your own origional clothes?! Besides making art, I also love watching it. I spend some of my free time going to different museums and exploring new exhibits.

Best Times

One of my favorite artwork is one that I completed during high school. My art class entered a competition called Recycle the Runway. The only rule for the competition is to create three pieces made out of recycleable materials. My class was split up into three groups, with each team having different themes. My team decided to create a dress called Nature. We were so happy that we won second place!

From what I can remember, the leaves were made out of soda cans. The chained mini skirt was created by folding soda lids while the long skirt was cardboard paper. My teammates were extremely creative, and came up with the idea of melting plastic to form flowers and stick on the body of the skirt.
