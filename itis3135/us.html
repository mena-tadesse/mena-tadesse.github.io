Nice To Meet You

Hey there, welcome to Teddy's – where car troubles meet solutions! We're not just your average auto shop; we're a bunch of car enthusiasts who love helping families and individuals keep their vehicles running smoothly. Our team is like one big happy family, and we treat every customer like they're a part of it too.

Our skilled technicians are not only great at what they do but also super approachable – you can trust us to treat your car like it's our own.

So, if you're looking for a reliable, trustworthy, and approachable neighborhood auto shop, you've come to the right place. Come on by, and let's get your wheels back on the road – and maybe share a few laughs along the way!
